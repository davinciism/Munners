About us

We, at Munners.Inc, are a group of developers. Through our skills, we aim to develop a social workplace for all Munners to connect. The workplace is a platform for sharing one's thoughts and ideas, and everything else that a fellow Munner can benefit from.

Our vision

We aim to provide MUNs with a wide range of toolsets that benefit their presence in this community with easy management and conduction of their MUN, and also a platform for their healthy competition as well as a means of connecting a Munner to the MUNs.
